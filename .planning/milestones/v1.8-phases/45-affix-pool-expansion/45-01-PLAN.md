# Plan 45-01: New Stat Types + Affix Definitions

## Objective

Add BLEED_CHANCE, POISON_CHANCE, and BURN_CHANCE to the StatType enum in tag.gd, then add all 14 new affixes (6 prefixes + 8 suffixes) to item_affixes.gd, replacing the relevant disabled stubs. AFF-04 (Evade suffix) is DROPPED per user decision -- evasion is already covered by existing prefixes.

## Context

- Phase 42 already added FLAT_SPELL_DAMAGE, INCREASED_SPELL_DAMAGE, INCREASED_CAST_SPEED, BLEED_DAMAGE, POISON_DAMAGE, BURN_DAMAGE, and CHAOS_RESISTANCE to the StatType enum. Only the three chance stat types are missing.
- Disabled stubs at lines 243-255 of item_affixes.gd include Cast Speed, Damage over time, and Bleed Damage. These are replaced with proper implementations. The Evade stub stays disabled (AFF-04 dropped). Sigil, Physical Reduction, Magical Reduction, Dodge Chance, and Dmg Suppression Chance remain disabled for future phases.
- Tag filtering uses OR-match: an item can roll an affix if ANY of its valid_tags appears in the affix's tags. Spell affixes (SPELL tag) only roll on SapphireRing. Bleed (PHYSICAL tag) rolls on STR+DEX weapons. Poison (CHAOS tag) rolls on DEX weapons + JadeRing. Burn (FIRE tag) has no current item with FIRE valid_tag -- these are pre-wired for Phase 47 INT weapons.
- DoT affixes are pre-wired: they roll on items and display stats, but DoT tick mechanics ship in Phase 48.
- Flat damage affixes use range params (dmg_min_lo/hi, dmg_max_lo/hi). DoT flat damage values are lower than direct damage (2-3/4-6 range vs 3-5/7-10 for physical).
